Recipes - World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adapted this recpie from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Granger. Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's restaurant in Kings Cross,
Sydeny. I have been after the recipe I have since transformed it into what i really believe are the best scrambled eggs I have ever tasted

This recipe is what i call a "very special breakfast", just look at the ingredients to see why it has to be believed

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream( 2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, cream, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a high heat (taking care not to burn the butter).
  3. Pour egg mixture into pan and wait until it starts settling around the edge of the pan (around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture into the center as if it were an omelet , and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold contents in again, leave for 20 seconds, and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  6. Grind a light sprinking of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chves.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan.
